Embed code for advisors

Choose how you want to add the Folio assessment to your website. Replace YOUR-FIRM-ID with your unique firm ID from your dashboard URL.

1

Button link (simplest)

Add a button anywhere on your site that opens the assessment. Works with any website — just paste the HTML where you want the button to appear.

<!-- Folio Assessment Button --> <a href="https://folioooooooo.netlify.app/assess.html?firm=YOUR-FIRM-ID" target="_blank" style="display:inline-block;padding:14px 28px;background:#2f8a5f;color:#fff; text-decoration:none;border-radius:999px;font-weight:600;font-size:15px; font-family:sans-serif"> Get your free financial assessment → </a>
Where to put it: Your homepage hero, about page, contact page, or anywhere you want prospects to take action. The button opens the assessment in a new tab.
2

Embedded iframe (seamless)

Embed the full assessment directly on your website page — prospects never leave your site. Paste this where you want the assessment to appear on the page.

<!-- Folio Assessment Embed --> <iframe src="https://folioooooooo.netlify.app/assess.html?firm=YOUR-FIRM-ID" width="100%" height="900" frameborder="0" style="border-radius:16px;border:1px solid rgba(0,0,0,0.08)" title="Financial Health Assessment"> </iframe>
Best for: Dedicated landing pages like yoursite.com/assessment. Works on Squarespace, Wix, WordPress, Webflow, and any site that lets you add custom HTML.
3

Popup on button click (advanced)

A button that opens the assessment in a popup overlay — keeps prospects on your page and feels premium. Paste this before your closing </body> tag.

<!-- Folio Assessment Popup --> <style> .folio-overlay{display:none;position:fixed;inset:0;background:rgba(0,0,0,0.6);z-index:9999;align-items:center;justify-content:center} .folio-overlay.open{display:flex} .folio-modal{background:#fff;border-radius:20px;width:90%;max-width:820px;height:88vh;overflow:hidden;position:relative;box-shadow:0 32px 80px rgba(0,0,0,0.25)} .folio-modal iframe{width:100%;height:100%;border:none} .folio-close{position:absolute;top:16px;right:16px;width:36px;height:36px;border-radius:50%;background:rgba(0,0,0,0.15);border:none;cursor:pointer;font-size:18px;color:#fff;display:flex;align-items:center;justify-content:center;z-index:1} .folio-close:hover{background:rgba(0,0,0,0.3)} </style> <!-- Trigger button (place anywhere on page) --> <button onclick="document.getElementById('folioOverlay').classList.add('open')" style="padding:14px 28px;background:#2f8a5f;color:#fff;border:none; border-radius:999px;font-weight:600;font-size:15px;cursor:pointer"> Get your free financial assessment → </button> <!-- Popup overlay --> <div class="folio-overlay" id="folioOverlay" onclick="if(event.target===this)this.classList.remove('open')"> <div class="folio-modal"> <button class="folio-close" onclick="document.getElementById('folioOverlay').classList.remove('open')">✕</button> <iframe src="https://folioooooooo.netlify.app/assess.html?firm=YOUR-FIRM-ID" title="Financial Assessment"></iframe> </div> </div>
Works on: Any website. Click outside the popup or the ✕ to close it. The assessment results are still sent to your Folio dashboard automatically.
