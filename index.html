Use cases

Built for three problems advisors face every day

Inbound prospecting

Turn website visitors into pre-qualified leads — without cold outreach, paid ads, or chasing strangers.

  • Prospects self-select by completing the assessment
  • Know their financial health score before you call
  • Prioritize hot leads automatically by score
  • No more wasted discovery calls
⚡

Faster client onboarding

Advisors spend 15% of their time on prospecting and intake. That time is better spent on advice that grows AUM.

  • Replace intake questionnaires with a branded assessment
  • Full financial snapshot arrives before the first meeting
  • Walk in knowing income, debt, surplus, and goals
